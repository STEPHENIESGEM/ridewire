# Rejected Proposals

This document tracks proposals that were considered but rejected as out of scope for RideWire.

## War/Conflict Framework (December 22, 2025)

**Proposal**: A comprehensive framework including:
- Chain Prompt survey for crisis scenarios
- War & Help perception survey
- Capability Assessment for conflict situations
- Situation & Capability Dashboard for crisis management
- De-Escalation & Empathy Brainstorm

**Decision**: **REJECTED**

**Reason**: RideWire is a business intelligence and automation platform focused on commercial enterprises. Military, defense, conflict management, and war-related frameworks are explicitly outside the project scope.

**Related Issues**: #2, #3, #4, #5, #6, #7

**Alternative**: Users interested in crisis management, conflict resolution, or emergency response frameworks should consider:
- Dedicated emergency management platforms
- Government/NGO crisis response systems
- Community resilience organizations
- Specialized conflict resolution projects

**Status**: Closed - Will not be implemented
